Academic Programs

The undergraduate programs at UOD are designed to help students develop the knowledge and capabilities needed to meet the challenges of modern society. An MIT education joins the power of a specific discipline to a concern for social values and goals. In addition to developing expertise in a given field, undergraduates are encouraged to take advantage of the opportunities for broad learning at MIT and to become creative, intellectual leaders and problem solvers whose passion for learning is lifelong.
